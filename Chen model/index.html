Research Direction

I am a Ph.D Candidate from Medvedev Group. A brief introduction of the research directions in our group can be found in this link: Research Introduction

My research interests are mainly design algorithm and data structure, parallel algorithm and machine learning method for bioinformatics problems. The bioinformatics problem I am currently working on is about genomic variants(SNP, small INDEL).

I believe computational methods are promising tech for solving problems in biomedical sciences.
