Research experience

2013 - present, Dr. Gong Chen's lab, Penn State University

The major goal of my research is to find an optmized small molecule formula to reprogram human astrocytes to functional neurons.

  • Screened small molecule combinations for in vitro human astrocyte-neuron conversion. Developed small molecule formula for both glutamatergic and GABAergic neuron conversion. Characterized maturation and function by performing electrophysiology and immunohistochemistry. Explored mechanism of astrocyte-neuron conversion by RT-PCR and immunohistochemistry.

  • Participated in delivering drugs into mouse brain by intracranial or intraperitoneal injection, trying to find a way to achieve in vivo conversion.

  • Developed Parkinson's Disease mouse model using 6-OHDA, and explored the conversion effect of different transcriptional factors.

  • Participated in functional recovery of cortex after stroke by delivering NeuroD1 in vivo to achieve astrocyte-neuron conversion.

    • 2010 - 2013, Undergraduate Research, Dr. Jiang-Ning Zhou's lab, USTC
